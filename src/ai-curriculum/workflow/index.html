Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior AI/ML experience?
No formal AI/ML background required. We'll cover the integration patterns and model-choice tradeoffs you need to ship a serious system. If you've never touched ML, that's fine, you'll lean into LLM/API-driven designs first.
What level of Rust do I need?
You should be comfortable writing Rust day-to-day: ownership, traits, error handling, and basic async/concurrency. This is alumni-only by design so we can move fast and stay system-focused.
What kinds of projects are appropriate?
Anything with a real user, a real data flow, and a real AI surface: RAG services, agentic backends, retrieval pipelines, inference proxies, ML feature stores, AI-augmented dev tools. Your week-1 1-on-1 is dedicated to scoping the right one.
What if I can't attend a live session?
All sessions are recorded. You'll still get value from async Discord support and your two 1-on-1s, but we recommend at least one of the two weekly calls live. That's where the cohort momentum lives.
Can my project be for my current employer?
Yes, and many students choose to. We'll help you scope it so it's both shippable in 21 days and a legitimate portfolio asset you can talk about externally (within whatever constraints you have).
Is this about getting an AI job, a promotion, or building a product?
All three. The final 1-on-1 is explicitly built around mapping your project to roles, promotion narrative, or a client/product pitch. Your call.
Is there a risk-free guarantee?
Yes. We offer a 7-day risk-free guarantee. If you join the cohort and decide it isn't the right fit within the first 7 days, you can opt out and get a full refund. No questions asked.
