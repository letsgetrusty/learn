How to Prepare for the Call:
Before we meet, take 20–30 minutes to reflect on the following:
1
What's your goal?
Where do you want to be in the next 6 months? Be specific. Whether it's landing your first Rust job, crafting a portfolio, or building a Rust startup...
2
What's stopping you?
What's missing for you to achieve your goals? Is it lack of structure? No accountability? Too much starting and stopping? What is it? Write them down.
3
What needs to change?
More tutorials probably aren't the answer. Be open to the idea that what you think you need — more coding time, more reading — may not be the most effective.
